Misson Statement

The South Asian Student Association's mission is to celebrate and teach others about South Asian culture at NEST+m through social events, presentations and cultural exchanges.

South Asian Communities in New York

South Asians have created small communities to have a place reminding them of thier culture in New York. These communities are located in South Asian culture-filled areas such as Jackson Heights or Astoria. Within these areas, there are places that each South Asian person holds close to thier heart, despite whether or not the place still exists.

Jackson Heights is one of the places where a lot of South Asians find home in. South Asians from the tri-state area all know of this place and come here to have South Asian food, get cultural attire,etc. Due to how rich South Asian culture is, we found it necessary to have a club where South Asians can have a place where they can acknowledge and celebrate thier culture.
